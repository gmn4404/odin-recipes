Carrot Cupcakes

Descripction

Carrot cupcake is a moist and spiced mini cake made with grated carrots and yogurt, which gives it a soft texture and light flavor. Itâ€™s sweet, aromatic, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 150g Carrots
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Dates or 1 Banana
  • 40g Flour
  • 1 Cinamon spoon
  • 8g Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Blend all the ingredients and divide the mixture into molds lightly greased with coconut or olive oil.
  2. Bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees.
  3. Let them cool completely and carefully remove the cupcakes from the molds.
  4. Lightly dip them in yogurt and sprinkle a little more cinnamon on top.
