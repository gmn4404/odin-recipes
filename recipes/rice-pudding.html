Rice Pudding

Description

Rice pudding is a creamy dessert made from rice slowly cooked with milk, sugar, and a touch of cinnamon or vanilla. Itâ€™s sweet, comforting, and often enjoyed warm or chilled.

Ingredients

  • 125g Risotto Rice
  • 1l Milk
  • 70g Sugar
  • 1 Cinamon stick
  • Little bit of the peel of a lemon

Steps

  1. Mix the rice, milk, peel of lemon and cinamon together on a pot.
  2. Put the pot on high fire, stiting well until is boiling.
  3. Lower the fire to slow and stir every 5-6 minutes during 1 hour (or is starting to get creamy).
  4. Add the sugar and stir during 1 minute so it doesn't get stuck
  5. Stir every 5 minutes for another 15 minutes.
  6. When creamy, remove from fire, take out the cinamon stick and the lem mon peel.
  7. Put the pudding in a container.
  8. Wait until is cooled down and store in the fridge.
  9. Preferably serve cold and with cinamon powder on top.
